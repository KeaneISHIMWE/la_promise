MORE ABOUT US

La Promise School has been a beacon of educational excellence since its founding in 2009. Located in Gisenyi, Rubavu, Western Province, the school started as a small institution with a strong vision: to provide quality education and nurture young minds into future leaders.

Early Beginnings (2009 - 2012) When La Promise School first opened its doors, it began with a handful of students and dedicated teachers who shared a passion for transforming education. Despite limited resources, the school quickly gained a reputation for its commitment to academic excellence and child-centered learning.

Growth and Expansion: (2013 - 2017) As the years progressed, La Promise School witnessed remarkable growth. More families entrusted their children to the school, leading to an expansion in both infrastructure and curriculum. Modern classrooms, a well-equipped library, and playgrounds were introduced to enhance the learning experience. The school also introduced extracurricular activities, including sports, music, and creative arts, ensuring that students develop holistically.

Achieving Excellence (2018 - Present) With a strong academic foundation, La Promise School has produced some of the best-performing students in national exams. The school has also built a reputation for instilling discipline, leadership skills, and innovation among its learners. Today, La Promise School continues to grow, shaping young minds with dedication, integrity, and excellence. It stands as a symbol of success in nursery and primary education, preparing students not just for the next level of education but for a bright future ahead.
